# Privacy Policy  
_Last updated: January 2025_

At **M.R. TechForge**, privacy is not a checkbox — it is part of building systems and relationships rooted in integrity.  
This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

---

## 1. Who We Are  
M.R. TechForge provides technology consulting, infrastructure hardening, and practical solutions for individuals and small organizations.

**Contact:**  
**Email:** [mark@mrtechforge.com](mailto:mark@mrtechforge.com)

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

### **Information you provide directly**  
When you contact us (e.g., email or web form), you may share:
- Your name  
- Your email address  
- Details about your request, systems, or project  

### **Basic website usage data**  
We may collect non-identifying technical data such as:
- Browser type  
- Device type  
- Approximate region (e.g., city/metro)  
- Pages visited and time spent on the site  

This helps us improve performance, content, and usability.

### **Optional subscription data**  
If you opt into any future mailing list or newsletter, we will collect the email address and preferences you provide.  
(We do not currently operate a newsletter.)

---

## 3. How We Use Information  
We use the information collected for:

- Responding to inquiries and providing services  
- Planning and improving consulting engagements  
- Maintaining site reliability and security  
- Sending updates or resources you explicitly request  

We do **not** use your data for advertising or profiling.

---

## 4. Cookies and Analytics  
This site may use lightweight analytics or cookies to understand how visitors use the site.

These tools:
- Measure traffic in aggregate (not personally identifiable)  
- Help us identify which pages are useful and where improvements are needed  

You may block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

---

## 5. Email & Direct Communication  
If you contact us, we will use the information solely to reply and follow up on your request.

If we ever send newsletters or updates:
- You will always have the ability to **unsubscribe**  
- We will only send messages you have opted into  

---

## 6. When We Share Information  
We do **not** sell your information.

We may share limited data only when necessary:

### **Service providers**  
With trusted third-party services (e.g., hosting, email delivery) who help operate the site.  
They are permitted to use information only to perform their tasks.

### **Legal obligations**  
If required to comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes.

### **Protection of rights**  
When needed to protect the safety, property, or rights of M.R. TechForge, clients, or others.

---

## 7. Data Retention  
We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

- Fulfill the purposes described in this policy  
- Comply with legal or accounting obligations  
- Resolve disputes  

If you request deletion of information we hold, we will make reasonable efforts to do so unless retention is legally required.

---

## 8. Security  
We employ reasonable security measures appropriate for a small consultancy, including:

- Secure hosting  
- Access controls  
- Good operational security practices  

No system is ever perfectly secure, but we aim to handle data with care and respect.

---

## 9. Your Rights  
Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

- Accessing the personal information we hold  
- Requesting corrections  
- Requesting deletion  
- Opting out of communications  

Regardless of legal jurisdiction, we will honor reasonable requests.

**To request access, updates, or deletion:**  
Email: [mark@mrtechforge.com](mailto:mark@mrtechforge.com)

## 10. Children’s Privacy  
This website is not directed at children under 13.  
We do not knowingly collect information from children.

If you believe information about a child has been provided to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

When we do, the “Last updated” date will change.
